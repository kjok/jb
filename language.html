Jeongbangsa temple is located 460 meters above sea level on the southern slope of Mt. Kumsu near Cheongpung Lake and Woraksan National Park. Woraksan was designated a “global park” in 2004 by the United Nations Environment Program and the World Conservation Monitoring Station. With a 1500 year history, this iconic temple has been an active prayer ground since its founding.

directions_car Directions from Seoul
  1. From the Jungang Expressway 55, take the South Jecheon IC exit near Jecheon City.
  2. Take a Right onto the Cheongpungho Highway 82 towards Cheongpung-myeon.
  3. Make a Left onto Cheonpung Bridge.
  4. After crossing the Neunggang Bridge , make a Left onto the Oksunbong 12 road.
  5. The temple will be at the end of this road.
directions_car Directions from Busan
  1. From the Jungang Expressway 55, take the North Danyang IC exit near the city of Danyan.
  2. Take the exit towards Hakhyeon-ri.
  3. After crossing the Neunggang Bridge, make a Left onto the Oksunbong 12 road.
  4. The temple will be at the end of this road.
directions_bus Public Transportation: Directions from Jecheon

We recommend taking the taxi from the Jecheon Bus Terminal instead of taking the local bus route.
